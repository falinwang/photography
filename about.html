About Me

Roy Fa-Lin Wang

"I establish the image of the world by reading and searching, learn people all over the globe with encounter and separation; by that, I can deeply learn the similarities and differences between people.”

Roy is a UX student from Taiwan who currently studies Master of Health Informatics program at the University of Michigan, School of Information and Public Health in Ann Arbor, Michigan. In 2016, she graduated with a BBA in Business Administration from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology. Her interests lie in the intersection of user experience (UX) design and research, front-end development, mHealth, and consumer health informatics. Roy is currently actively searching for Summer 2019 Internship.
